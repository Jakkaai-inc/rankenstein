What is minky fabric, and what is this made of?

Minky is a plush, low-pile polyester fabric with a faux-fur feel. This one is 100% polyester with a smooth 3 mm flat pile — shorter and flatter than dimple/dot minky, which makes it easier to sew and cut clean lines.
